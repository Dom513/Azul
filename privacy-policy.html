AzuGo – Privacy Policy

Effective date: October 8, 2025

AzuGo (“the App”) is developed and maintained by [Dominic Wilde] (“we”, “us”, or “our”). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use AzuGo.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

AzuGo does not collect, store, or share any personal information. We do not require users to create accounts, enter personal data, or provide contact information.

2. Permissions

The App may request certain device permissions to function properly (for example, audio, storage, or internet access), but:

  • These permissions are used only for gameplay and app functionality.
  • No data is transmitted or stored on our servers.
  • No analytics or advertising SDKs are included.

3. Children’s Privacy

AzuGo is suitable for general audiences and does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

4. Third-Party Services

AzuGo does not integrate with third-party analytics, ad networks, or external APIs that collect data. If future updates introduce third-party services, this policy will be updated to explain how those services handle user information.

5. Data Security

Because AzuGo does not collect or store personal data, there is no user information to secure. All game data (such as high scores or settings) is stored locally on your device.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, the updated version will be available within the app and on our official distribution page.
